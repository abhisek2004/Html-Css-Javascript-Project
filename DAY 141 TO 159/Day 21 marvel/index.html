Marvel

It was founded in 1939 as Timely Publications and later became known as Marvel Comics. Marvel is renowned for creating a vast array of iconic superhero characters, many of whom have become household names.

Thor

Based on the Norse god of thunder, Thor wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir and protects Earth as well as the realms of Asgard.

Captain America

Created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby, Captain America is Steve Rogers, a World War II soldier who is enhanced to the peak of human physicality and fights for justice using his iconic shield.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who builds a powered exoskeleton to become the armored superhero, Iron Man.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff is a skilled spy, assassin, and expert martial artist who plays a crucial role in S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Avengers.

Hawkeye

Hawkeye is known for his exceptional archery skills and is a skilled marksman, making him a valuable member of various superhero teams, including the Avengers.

hulk

Created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby, Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk, a massive, green-skinned creature with incredible strength, when he becomes angry or emotionally distressed.

Spiderman

Created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, Spider-Man is a high school student named Peter Parker who gains superpowers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He's known for his agility, web-swinging abilities, and his witty banter while fighting crime.

Doctor Strange

Dr. Stephen Strange becomes the Sorcerer Supreme after a car accident ends his surgical career and leads him to the mystical arts.

Captain Marvel

"Captain Marvel" is a fictional superhero appearing in comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was created by writer Roy Thomas and artist Gene Colan

Groot

Groot is a fictional character from Marvel Comics, known for being a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy, a group of interstellar superheroes.

Scarlet Witch

Scarlet Witch, whose real name is Wanda Maximoff, is a mutant with the ability to manipulate probability and reality using chaos magic. She can alter the fabric of reality itself, leading to a wide range of effects, including energy projection, telekinesis, and reality manipulation.
