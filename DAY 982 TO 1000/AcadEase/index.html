What does AcadEase do?

AcadEase is a time management hub built specially for B.Tech students. Organize classes, exams, assignments, club events, and doubt sessions – all on a single, smart dashboard.

  • 📚 Track resources, notes, and PYQs subject-wise.
  • 📝 Never miss a deadline with exam & assignment reminders.
  • ⏳ Study smarter using Pomodoro & study timers.
  • 🤝 Get doubts solved via student–student & student–teacher boards.

            Doubts

            Never stay stuck – get your doubts solved, one-on-one or with peers.

            Student–Teacher Dashboard

            Ask clearly, learn deeply – one-on-one doubt solving.

            • State the subject, topic, and where you are stuck.
            • Attach screenshots or code snippets if needed.
            • Be respectful of teacher time & slots.
            • Follow allotted doubt hours by each faculty.

            Student–Student Forum

            Discuss, debate, and learn together across all subjects.

            • Use tags like [DSA], [Maths], [CN], [OS].
            • No spamming, sharing exam papers, or rude behavior.
            • Upvote helpful answers & mark solutions.
            • Keep discussions topic-focused.
